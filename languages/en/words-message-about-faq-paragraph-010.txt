The padlock displayed in your browser indicates that snooping of your location is very difficult while you stay on the News Here Now website.  However, if you click a button that takes you to the Open Street Map website or to the satellite map, then your location is revealed to the other website, and your location might be intercepted by yet other computers if that website does not fully protect your privacy.  As another clarification, if you share the same computer with other users, the padlock does not protect your privacy from the other users.  For more information about privacy, click the Privacy Policy button at the bottom of this and every page.
